Services

Most businesses would use a collection agency for problem accounts if it were not for the high cost, the administrative hassles, and their reluctance to subject customers to the unpleasant nature of the traditional collection process.

With our 5-Star Collection Program, these drawbacks have been eliminated.

Our new approach begins with a low, flat-fee based collection cost. Unlike traditional agencies that charge a 50% collection commission, our cost of collection begins with a flat-fee of $12.00 or less per account, regardless of account balance. At this price, our cost of collection is less than the cost of your own in-house efforts, but as a collection agency, our collection rate is much higher.

Our new approach employs proprietary database software and internet technologies to make account management fast, easy, and available 24/7. No wasted staff time making copies, sending faxes and waiting for status reports that are outdated by the time they are received. Our Account Management Website provides on-demand account status reporting and agency performance reporting as needed, 24/7.

Our new approach is diplomatic and will not interfere with viable customer relationships. You can now utilize the power of a collection agency to improve cash flow and profitability without concern for the impact on your customer relationships.

Our new approach to collection services includes a commitment to delivery of services in a professional manner. As business owners ourselves, we understand the importance of your hard-earned reputation in the community. We pledge to each of our clients that we will do nothing in the performance of our duties that will reflect badly on either of our organizations.
